SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN BANGSAR

I studied primary school at Sekolah Kebangsaan, located on Jalan Pantai Bharu in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. In sixth grade, I was also the supervisor of the resource center. Sekolah Kebangsaan Bangsar is also nearby.

SMK SUNGAI BESAR

I study high school in SMK Sungai Besar in Sungai Besar, Sabak Bernam, Selangor, Malaysia. From 2016 to 2021, I attended school till I received my SPM certificate. After receiving my PT3 scores, I took the accountant and business course. Unbeknownst to me, time flew by, and I found myself in the middle of the SPM test, which gave our batch a dreadful sensation when we sat for the exam during COVID-19, but the exam carried on. I made many memories with my friends because they all went their ways after SPM to continue studying or working.
